About Me

I am currently a post-doctoral fellow in Applied Statistics at Université Laval in Québec City, Québec (Canada) under the supervision of Dr. Louis-Paul Rivest. I am a recent graduate in Applied Statistics at the University of Guelph in Guelph, Ontario (Canada). My Ph.D. advisors were Dr. Peter Kim and Dr. Tony Desmond. A copy of my dissertation can be found at the University of Guelph Institutional Repository (The Atrium).

I have been accredited as an Associate Statistician (AStat) by the Statistics Society of Canada (SSC) and I am an Associated Member (AMIMA) of the Institute of Mathematics and Its Applications (IMA) .

My math genealogy tree can be found here. This tree was created in R using code modified from the example provided by Francois Keck, and data from the Mathematics Genealogy Project .

My Erdös number is 3 (Verified using the Collaboration Distance Tool provided by MathSciNet).
