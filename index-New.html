About Me

Brief Introduction

I am a senior statistician at the Provincial Health Services Authority in Vancouver, British Columbia (Canada).

I am currently applying statistical concepts on projects related to:

  • SARS‑CoV‑2 virus (i.e. the virus that causes Covid-19)
  • Hematology
  • Other projects (classified)

Background

My formal training is in mathematics and statistics.

I completed my PhD in Applied Statistics from the University of Guelph under the supervision of Dr. Peter Kim and Dr. Tony Desmond.

My math genealogy tree can be found here. My Erdös number is 3 (Verified using the Collaboration Distance Tool provided by MathSciNet).

Coding

I code in a variety of languages, however I primarily use Python, R and SAS.

I am also a member on YYJ Tech Slack.

Professional Development

I am an Associate Statistician (A.STAT.) and an Associate Member of the Institute of Mathematics and its Applications (AMIMA) .

I am working towards obtaining the P.STAT. designation under the mentorship of Soyean Kim.
