Brief Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor (Teaching Stream) in Statistics with the Department of Mathematical and Computational Sciences at the University of Toronto at Mississauga in Mississauga, ON, Canada.

Coindidentally, the University of Toronto at Mississauga is also the same university I completed my bachelor's degree.

My formal training is in mathematics and statistics.

Background

I completed my PhD in Applied Statistics from the University of Guelph under the supervision of Dr. Peter Kim and Dr. Tony Desmond.

My math genealogy tree can be found here (Created with data gathered from the Mathematics Genealogy Project).

My Erdös number is 3 and my Dijkstra number is 6 (Verified using the Collaboration Distance Tool provided by MathSciNet).

My dissertation can be found here. My PhD research was related to providing a unifying theory for the relationship between some broad classes of density functions over the hyper-surface \(\mathbb{S}^{p-1}\).
