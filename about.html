About Me

Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor (Teaching Stream) in Statistics with the Department of Mathematical and Computational Sciences at the University of Toronto at Mississauga in Mississauga, ON, Canada.

I am also a Professional Statistician (PStat).

Background

I completed my PhD in Applied Statistics from the University of Guelph under the supervision of Dr. Peter Kim and Dr. Tony Desmond.

My doctoral research was related to providing a unifying theory for the relationship between some broad classes of density functions over the hyper-surface \(\mathbb{S}^{p-1}\). My dissertation can be found here.
